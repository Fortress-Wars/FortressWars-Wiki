# Rules

All players are accountable to the rules regardless if they have read them. Punishments are entirely up to the discretion of the staff team. Updates to rules will be posted in the [changelog](#changelog) below.

---

## **1. Chat Behavior**

**1.1 Disrespect**

- Do not disrespect or harass other people.

**1.2 Derogatory Comments**

- Offensive language and derogatory comments are not allowed. This includes but is not limited to racism, death threats, suicide encouragement, anti-LGBTQ+ jokes, etc.

**1.3 Spamming**

- Spamming and excessive capitalization is prohibited. What constitutes spam is up to the discretion of the staff team.

**1.4 Advertising**

- No advertising other Minecraft or Discord servers.

**1.5 Use of Channels**

- Use the appropriate Discord channels and forums. This includes voice chats and ticket support.

**1.6 Inappropriate Content**

- NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content and politics are not allowed on this server. Staff reserve the right to determine what constitutes inappropriate content.

**1.7 Toxicity**

- Excessive toxicity is not allowed.

**1.8 Doxing**

- Spreading any player’s personal information is prohibited. This includes names, addresses, real-life pictures, phone numbers, etc.

**1.9 Impersonation**

- Impersonating other people, including players and staff members, is prohibited.

**1.10 Malicious Links**

- Malicious links are not allowed. This includes, but is not limited to, IP grabbers or similar tools.

---

## **2. Gameplay Behavior**

**2.1 Chat**

- All chat-related rules apply in-game.

**2.2 Cheating**

- Cheating of any kind is prohibited. Anything that provides an unfair advantage is not allowed.

**2.3 Farming**

- Farming credits and/or statistics is prohibited. This includes using alternate accounts to boost stats or credits. Playing defensively, supporting teammates, or performing well does not automatically constitute farming.

**2.4 Exploiting**

- Exploiting bugs or unintended mechanics is prohibited and must be reported using the `#ticket-creation` channel in the [Official Discord](https://discord.fortresswars.net).

**2.5 Assisting in Exploiting**

- Knowingly allowing or assisting another player in exploiting is prohibited and may result in punishment.

---

## **3. Tournament Rules**

All tournament participants are expected to uphold competitive integrity and sportsmanship. Teams or players that violate these rules may be disqualified from the tournament.

**3.1 Eligibility**

- Players must have a Discord account to participate.
- Players may only participate on **one team per tournament**.
- Alternate accounts are strictly prohibited.

**3.2 Teams & Staff Participation**

- Team formats are announced per tournament.
- Staff members may participate, but **only one staff member per team** is allowed.

**3.3 Sportsmanship**

- Treat players, staff, and spectators with respect at all times.
- Harassment, targeted abuse, or disruptive behavior is prohibited.
- Unsportsmanlike conduct may result in penalties or removal from the tournament.

**3.4 Fair Play**

- Cheats, hacks, macros, scripts, or unauthorized modifications are strictly prohibited.
- Exploiting tournament-specific mechanics is not allowed.
- Any discovered exploits must be reported immediately to tournament staff.

**3.5 Match Integrity**

- Players may only play on their registered account.
- Account sharing or playing under another player’s name is prohibited.
- Only registered players and approved substitutes may participate.
- Match fixing, intentional feeding, or griefing is prohibited.

**3.6 Communication & Compliance**

- Team Captains are responsible for communication with tournament staff.
- Teams must follow instructions from the Host or Co-Host during matches.
- Failure to comply with staff instructions may result in penalties.

**3.7 Streaming & Media**

- Stream sniping or using live broadcasts for competitive advantage is prohibited.
- Official tournament streams must include at least a 2 minute delay.
- Players may not interfere with or disrupt official broadcasts.

**3.8 Enforcement & Penalties**
Tournament staff may issue penalties at their discretion, including:

- Warnings
- Map forfeits
- Match forfeits
- Player suspension
- Team disqualification

**3.9 Zero Tolerance Violations**
The following may result in immediate disqualification:

- Cheating or hacking
- Hate speech or severe harassment
- Threats or doxxing
- Repeated or severe rule violations

---

## **4. Other Rules**

**4.1 Wasting Staff’s Time**  
Do not waste staff time. Abuse of Discord features may result in loss of access to those features.

**4.2 Support**  
Use the `#ticket-creation` channel in the [Official Discord](https://discord.fortresswars.net) instead of messaging staff directly.

**4.3 Evading Punishment**  
Evading punishment or using methods to bypass enforcement is prohibited and may result in extended penalties.

**4.4 Common Sense & Loopholing**  
Use common sense. Staff may take action against behavior not explicitly listed here.
