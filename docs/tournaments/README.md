# Tournaments

The Fortress Wars Tournament is a seasonal, competitive 4v4 league designed to showcase team coordination, strategy, and mechanical skill.

## Tournament Format

### Teams

- 4v4
- Roster Size:
  - Main Players: 4
  - Substitutes: 0–2
- Each team must designate:
  - Team Captain (Discord required)
  - Team Co-Captain (Discord required)

---

## League Structure

### Season (Seeding Phase)

- Format: Round Robin or Swiss
- Purpose: Playoff seeding
- Match Length: Best of 1 (1 map)
- Maps:
  - Selected by Staff
  - Alternates between:
    - Classic (No Build)
    - King of the Hill (KOTH)\*

### Playoffs

- Format: Single Elimination
- Match Length: Best of 3
- Maps selected through in-game ban/pick system

---

## Map Selection

### Map Pool

- 9–11 total maps
- All maps must be tournament-approved

### Home / Away

- Red = Home
- Blue = Away

### Playoff Ban/Pick Order

1. Red bans
2. Blue bans
3. Red picks
4. Blue picks
5. Red bans
6. Blue bans
7. Red picks
8. Blue picks

_(All playoff matches use Best of 3 maps)_

---

## Game Rules

### Tournament Rule Set

- Damage: 3
- Support: 1
- Tank: 1
- Utility: 1

## Player Requirements

- Minimum players to start a match: 3
- Maximum players per match: 4
- Substitutions are allowed between maps, not mid-map

## Scheduling and Match Times

### Default Match Times

- Thursday: 6:00 PM EST
- Friday: 6:00 PM EST
- Saturday: 6:00 PM EST
- Sunday: 6:00 PM EST

### Rescheduling

- Both teams must agree on a new time
- The new time must be approved by the Host or Co-Host
- If no agreement is reached:
  - The original scheduled time stands

---

## Match Oversight and Streaming

- A Host or Co-Host must be present for every match
- Matches should be live streamed whenever possible
- Streams must include at least a `2 minute` delay

---

## Rules and Conduct

- All participants in Fortress Wars Tournaments are expected to uphold a high standard of sportsmanship and integrity. By registering, teams and players agree to comply with the [rules](/rules/).
