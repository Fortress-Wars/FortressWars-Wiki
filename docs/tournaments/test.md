# Tournament Forfeit, No-Show, & Disconnect Rules

**Season 1**

These rules apply specifically to Fortress Wars Tournament **Season 1** and are enforced in addition to all global rules and the Tournament Code of Conduct.

All teams are responsible for being ready to play at their scheduled match time.

---

## 1. Match Start Requirements

- Teams are expected to be ready **at the scheduled match time**.
- A Host or Co-Host will initiate match setup.
- Teams must field a minimum of **3 players** to begin a match.

---

## 2. No-Show Rules

### 2.1 Definition

A team is considered a **no-show** if:

- Fewer than 3 players are present **10 minutes** after the scheduled start time.
- The team does not respond to staff communication attempts.

### 2.2 Penalties

- **Season Matches (Seeding Phase):**
  - Match recorded as a **loss**
- **Playoff Matches:**
  - Match recorded as a **forfeit loss**
- Repeated no-shows may result in:
  - Removal from the tournament
  - Disqualification from future seasons

---

## 3. Forfeit Rules

### 3.1 Voluntary Forfeit

- Teams may voluntarily forfeit a match by notifying tournament staff.
- Forfeits must be communicated **before match start** whenever possible.

### 3.2 Effects of Forfeit

- Season matches count as a **loss**
- Playoff matches result in **immediate elimination**
- Excessive forfeits may impact future tournament eligibility

---

## 4. Disconnect (DC) Rules

### 4.1 Pre-Match Disconnects

- Disconnects before the match starts do not count toward penalties.
- The match will not begin until both teams are ready or the no-show timer expires.

### 4.2 In-Game Disconnects

- If a player disconnects during a map:
  - The map **continues**
  - Substitutions are allowed **between maps**
- Teams may play down a player, provided they meet the minimum player requirement.

### 4.3 Server Issues

- If a disconnect is caused by a **server-side issue**, staff may:
  - Restart the map
  - Reschedule the match
  - Void the map result

Staff discretion applies in all cases.

---

## 5. Abuse & Exploitation

- Intentional disconnecting to gain advantage is prohibited.
- Fake disconnects, stalling, or abuse of these rules may result in:
  - Map forfeits
  - Match forfeits
  - Tournament disqualification

---

## 6. Staff Discretion

Tournament staff reserve the right to:

- Interpret these rules
- Resolve edge cases
- Make rulings in the interest of competitive integrity

All staff decisions are **final**.

---

## Versioning

**Season 1**

- Initial release of forfeit, no-show, and disconnect rules
- Effective immediately for all Season 1 tournament matches
